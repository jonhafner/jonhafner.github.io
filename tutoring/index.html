Jon Hafner’s Tutoring Info

Qualifications and Experience: I have tutored in some capacity off and on for roughly two decades. In recent years, my primary employment has been as a part-time instructor in the Department of Mathematics at The University of Akron, from which I received my M.S. (Master of Science degree) in 2012. I also taught at the Western Campus of the Cuyahoga Community College for a year. Previous to that, I worked for four years primarily as a tutor for the ACT, SAT, and other standardized exams.

The courses I have taught at UA or Tri-C include:

  • Math for Everyday Life / Contemporary Math
  • Intermediate Algebra
  • College Algebra
  • Precalculus
  • Intro to Discrete Math
  • Calculus with Business Applications
  • Calculus I–III
  • Differential Equations

I have also tutored students in a variety of other courses, including Linear Algebra, Applied Numerical Methods, and other topics ranging from basic arithmetic up through fourth-year undergraduate mathematics. Furthermore, I have occasionally tutored adjacent subjects, including computer science, statistics, physics, and chemistry. Those are outside my specialty, but I can usually be helpful at the introductory level.

Fees: My rate is $50/hour. I offer a no-risk guarantee for the first hour: if it isn’t working out, we can stop there at no charge to you. (No one has yet exercised that option, but I continue to offer it for potential clients’ peace of mind.)

Medium: When not in the middle of a global pandemic, I prefer to meet with students in person, but since that is not currently an option, I have temporarily switched to online instruction. Now that Google Meet is free for everyone, that has become my preferred medium. I have also used GoToMeeting, though it is slightly less ideal. I am open to trying other platforms if there is another one you prefer. (The chief exception is that I cannot use FaceTime, as I have no Apple devices.)

Payment methods: I accept Google Pay, PayPal, checks, and money orders. (I also accept cash, of course, but that could be difficult to safely transfer currently. I cannot accept Venmo due to its policies on business transactions. I also cannot accept Apple Pay because, again, I have no Apple devices.)

Scheduling: Inquire for availability. I am generally pretty flexible with scheduling and rescheduling, within the bounds of my availability.
