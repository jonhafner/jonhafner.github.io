Jon Hafner’s Tutoring Info

Qualifications and Experience: I have an M.S. in Mathematics, and I primarily work as a university math instructor. I have tutored in some capacity on and off for over two decades.

The courses I have taught at a university or college include:

  • Math for Everyday Life / Contemporary Math
  • Intermediate Algebra
  • College Algebra / Algebra for Calculus
  • Precalculus
  • Calculus with Business Applications
  • Analytic Geometry–Calculus I–III
  • Intro to Ordinary Differential Equations
  • Intro to Discrete Mathematics
  • Basic Probability and Statistics

I have also tutored students in a variety of other courses, including Linear Algebra, Applied Numerical Methods, and other topics ranging from basic arithmetic up through fourth-year undergraduate mathematics. Furthermore, I have occasionally tutored adjacent subjects, including computer science, physics, and chemistry. Those are outside my specialty, but I can usually be helpful at the introductory level.

I also worked at a learning center for four years, primarily as a tutor for the ACT, SAT, and other standardized exams.

Fees: My rate is $60/hour. I offer a no-risk guarantee for the first hour: if it isn’t working out, we can stop there at no charge to you. (No one has yet exercised that option, but I continue to offer it for potential clients’ peace of mind.)

If I must drive a significant distance, I may need to also charge a per-session travel fee to cover my transportation expenses. Inquire for details.

Medium: I can meet with students in person or online. The former is usually more effective, while the latter tends to be more convenient and flexible. I typically conduct in-person sessions at a client’s home, or at a library, café, or some other public place. For online meetings, I usually use Google Meet, but I’m open to trying other platforms if there is another one you prefer.

Payment methods: I accept cash, checks, Zelle, PayPal, Google Pay, and possibly other methods upon request. (Unless their policies have changed recently, I cannot accept Venmo or Apple Pay due to their restrictions.)

Scheduling: Inquire for current availability. I’m generally pretty flexible with scheduling and rescheduling, within the bounds of my availability.
