  • § 4.2: Exponential Functions

    • Page 355 includes a restatement of several fundamental properties of exponents and directs the reader to Appendix I.C for a more thorough review. I will not review these in class: you are expected to be intimately familiar with them by now. There are very good and simple reasons why they are true, so if they are not transparent to you, you should either read that appendix carefully (just part A, pp. A-14 – A-17) or ask me about them outside of class, when I’ll be happy to break them down for you. This is true regardless of how good you may or may not be at remembering and applying them: it is all too easy to find oneself applying by rote techniques that seem to work by “magic”!
