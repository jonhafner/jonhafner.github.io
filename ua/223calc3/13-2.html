Calculus III

§ 13.2: Derivatives and Integrals of Vector Functions

Suggested Reading

Entire section. Take note of the differentiation rules in Theorem 3: Properties 1 and 2 together tell us that differentiation of vector functions is a linear operatior, just like summations, limits, and the differentiation and integration of real-valued functions. Properties 3–5 are product rules, one for each form of multiplication we have with vectors. And as noted, Property 6 is a chain rule.
