Calculus III

§ 15.4: Applications of Double Integrals

Suggested Reading

In this course, we focus only on the subsections “Density and Mass” and “Moments and Centers of Mass”. (For some useful background on the latter, see § 8.3.) The other sections are optional: they are useful and important applications, but we do not emphasize them in this course. I’ve never seen them appear on a final exam, and I never test them myself. However, they are useful and interesting, so I encourage you to at least skim these subsections and their corresponding exercises. If any seem applicable to your studies or interests, give them a try.
