Calculus III

§ 16.5*: Divergence and Curl

This is the section I’ve altered the most, and the only one I’ve moved to a different position in the sequence. The presentation of divergence and curl is the biggest point of disagreement I have with Stewart. Because of this, I don’t recommend reading this section until after we’ve finished covering it in class. Once that’s done, feel free to consult the textbook for additional information and examples.

Note that the last part of this section (just over a page), titled “Vector Forms of Green’s Theorem”, addresses material from § 16.4, which we have not covered yet. Ignore that for now; I will cover this material in § 16.4*.
