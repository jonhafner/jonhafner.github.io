Assigned Homework Problem

Let \(R\) be the parallelogram with vertices \((0,0)\), \((4,1)\), \((3,3)\), and \((7,4)\). Use the change of variables \(x = 4u + 3v\), \(y = u + 3v\) to compute \( \iint_R e^{4x-y} \,dA\). (Hint: If you do the conversion correctly, your new region should be quite nice!)
