Assigned Homework Problem

Evaluate \[ \int_{-1}^{0} \int_{- \sqrt3 \,x}^{\sqrt{4-x^2}} \sqrt{1+x^2+y^2} \,dy\,dx + \int_{0}^{\sqrt2} \int_{x}^{\sqrt{4-x^2}} \sqrt{1+x^2+y^2} \,dy\,dx. \] (Hint: You should be able to write this as just one double integral in polar coördinates! You may find it helpful to sketch a graph of the region of integration.)
