§ 16.1: Vector Fields

This is a relatively short and straightforward section, mostly spent drawing pictures of vector fields and calculating more gradients. Read the text and carefully examine the figures; I don’t really have anything of value to add. The exercises should be reasonably straightforward if you understand the concepts here—if you encounter difficulty or have any questions, let me know!

(If the brief mention of conservative fields and potential functions is unclear, you can ignore it for now. I don’t actually know why they introduce these terms at this point, since as far as I can tell, we don’t do anything with them until § 16.3. I’ll explain more about them then.)
