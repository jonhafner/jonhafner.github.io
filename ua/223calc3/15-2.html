Calculus III

§ 15.2: Double Integrals over General Regions

Suggested Reading

Entire section. Don’t worry about memorizing the various technical formulae. In particular, you won’t be asked to classify a region as “type I” or “type II”—you’ll just need to know how to recognize which way you should set up an integral.

Exercises

Departmental suggestions: #1–14, 17–40, 47–50, 55–66

Recall that (to the best of my knowledge) you won’t be graded on 3D sketches, so #47–50 are just for the purposes of developing valuable skills. Don’t worry if the results aren’t pretty—their value is solely for helping you visualize situations.

If you want additional practice with some longer and more complex problems, #43–46 would be useful additions.
