§ 16.8: Stokes’ Theorem

This is a short section; read the text, and you may also find the video in the eBook to be helpful. Much like Green’s Theorem, Stokes’ Theorem tells us that a field’s circulation around a loop is its total rotation throughout the surface it bounds. I work through a couple examples in “§ 16.8a: Exercise #4” (YouTube | Panopto) and “§ 16.8b: Exercise #8” (YouTube | Panopto).
