Entire section. The latter portion, on functions, should be mostly familiar by this point, but it’s still valuable to see some of the concepts and technicalities in the context of relations. Exercises

After that, #9–10 are particularly good exercises to try. #11–20 are mostly focused on functions; these could be good to work on to prepare for the final exam, but if you’re only focused on relations for now, you can hold off on them for the time being.
