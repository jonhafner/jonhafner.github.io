§ 2.4: Application: Digital Logic Circuits

Suggested Reading

Entire section. In the closing subsection, note that the use of the Sheffer stroke | for NAND conflicts with an alternative notation for OR that I supplied in § 2.1: the vertical bar | is used for OR in many common programming languages, including most of those which are C-like. This is an excellent example of the prevalence of conflicting notations, the importance of always checking the conventions of whatever context you’re working in, and the need to be adaptable to different circumstances. Be sure to seek understanding of the underlying concepts rather than relying purely on memorization of specific representations.
