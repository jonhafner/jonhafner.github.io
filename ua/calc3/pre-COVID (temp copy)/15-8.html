Assigned Homework Problem

Let \(R\) be the region above the \(xy\)-plane bounded below by the cone \(z^2 = x^2 + y^2\) and between the spheres \(x^2 + y^2 + z^2 = 4\) and \(x^2 + y^2 + z^2 = 9\). Compute the integral of \(f(x,y,z) = (x^2 + y^2 + z^2)^{-2} \) over this region.
