§ 12.2: Vectors

Suggested Reading

Entire section. Note that the Properties of Vectors in the box on p. 840 (after Example 4) tell us that vector addition and scalar multiplication are associative, commutative, and distributive, 0 is the additive identity, and 1 is the multiplicative identity. This is analogous to the algebra of real numbers.
