Final Exam

Schedule: The final exam will be held on Tuesday, May 6, from 7:45–9:45 a.m. in ASEC 122. Notify me immediately if you have any irreconcilable conflicts so we can arrange an alternative time for you.

Laplace table: The last page of your exam will consist of this table of Laplace transforms. The arrangement is a bit different from the one used on our Test 5, and many of the more complicated forms are omitted, but all the relevant entries are there.

Reference sheet: You are permitted to bring a reference sheet to the exam, on which you may write whatever you wish. It must fit on a single side of one sheet of standard US letter paper (8½″ × 11″). You may wish to include not only formulae, but also names and procedures of major methods, forms of different types of equations, etc. You will sometimes be instructed to apply a particular method, but most equations you are asked to solve will not provide any hints as to the expected technique!

Calculator: You are permitted to use a calculator of a type approved for this course, but none will be necessary if you are comfortable with basic arithmetic. (I didn’t have a calculator on hand when I took the exam to test its length, and I didn’t feel lacking.)
