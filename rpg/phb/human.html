Human

Exceptional Ability: Humans are known for their versatility and adaptability. Increase any two ability scores of your choice by one point each.

Languages: As a human, you speak the language of your homeland and Common. Bonus languages can include any language you could conceivably have had sufficient contact with. (Check with your DM if uncertain.) Typical choices include other nearby regional languages, as well as the languages of the major nonhuman races, such as dwarvish, elvish, goblin, giant, etc.
